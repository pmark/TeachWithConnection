# Project

A static professional website for Katie Statman-Weil and With Connection that helps early childhood organizations inquire about professional development workshops, keynotes, and consultation.

---

## Purpose

The site exists to position Katie Statman-Weil, EdD, LCSW as a credible, relationship-centered early childhood educator, trainer, consultant, speaker, clinician, and author. It should help schools, early childhood organizations, districts, conferences, and educational leaders understand her expertise and submit an inquiry for professional development, workshops, keynotes, or consultation.

The site should use the existing With Connection website as the source of truth for brand, language, proof, and reusable content. Therapy practice content may inform tone and credibility, but the new site's primary job is educator-facing professional training and speaking.

---

## Audience

**Primary audience:**
Early childhood organizations, schools, districts, and educational leaders who can hire Katie for professional development, workshops, staff training, consultation, or keynote speaking. They are likely trying to support teaching teams working with young children with complex behavior, trauma histories, neurodevelopmental differences, dysregulation, and classroom stress.

**Secondary audience:**
Conference planners looking for an early childhood keynote speaker or workshop presenter who can speak with warmth, rigor, and practical classroom relevance.

**Additional audience:**
Center directors and administrators who need consultation for a specific child, classroom, staff team, or organizational challenge.

**Supporting audience:**
Educators who may read articles, download free resources, share Katie's work internally, or influence a leader's decision to inquire.

---

## Goals

**The single most important outcome for a visitor:**
A qualified visitor submits an inquiry form about bringing Katie to their organization, school, district, conference, or staff team.

**Supporting goals:**

1. Establish Katie's credibility through her doctorate, LCSW credential, authorship, clinical expertise, educator background, consulting experience, testimonials, publications, and partner/client proof visible on the existing With Connection site.
2. Make the professional development offer clear, with workshops first, keynotes second, and consultation as a supporting service.
3. Present Katie's philosophy: relational, trauma-responsive, neuroaffirming, attachment-informed, regulation-focused, practical, and rooted in real classrooms.
4. Offer ungated free resources that demonstrate expertise, create goodwill, and route visitors gently toward inquiry.
5. Preserve With Connection brand continuity so the new site feels like part of the same trusted body of work.

---

## Service emphasis

The site should emphasize the offers in this priority order:

1. Professional development workshops and staff trainings for early childhood educators.
2. Keynotes for early childhood conferences, schools, and organizations.
3. Consultation for schools, centers, directors, and teaching teams.
4. Free downloadable resources as credibility and goodwill builders.
5. Thought leadership through selected blog posts and publication references.

- Doctorate in Education and Licensed Clinical Social Worker credentials.

- Experience as a preschool teacher, center director, early childhood college professor, early childhood mental health consultant, clinician, trainer, and consultant.

- Expertise in attachment, trauma-responsive care, neurodivergence, emotional regulation, child development, foster care/adoption, and relationship-centered behavior support.
- Practical classroom framing that helps educators move beyond compliance, punishment, crisis management, and generic strategies toward connection, regulation, prevention, and shared understanding.
- Ability to validate educators' emotional experience while offering concrete, usable tools.

## Resources strategy

Free resources should be ungated. Visitors should not have to join an email list or submit personal information to access them.

Resources should support business goals by:

- Demonstrating Katie's approach in a practical, useful format.
- Giving educators and leaders a low-friction way to experience her thinking before inquiring.
- Supporting SEO around early childhood behavior, trauma-responsive classrooms, regulation, neuroaffirming classrooms, and educator support.
- Including gentle calls to action such as "Bring this work to your team" or "Inquire about professional development" without turning resources into sales pages.

No dedicated public resource library was found in the current With Connection sitemap. Launch resources should either be created from existing With Connection materials or surfaced from off-site files supplied by the owner.

## Tone and personality

**This site should feel:**
Warm, grounded, professional, compassionate, practical, trustworthy, relational, trauma-informed, and non-judgmental.

Examples of the desired feel:

- Speak directly to educators who feel stuck, exhausted, or unsure without blaming them.
- Pair emotional validation with concrete next steps.
- Use Katie's clinical and academic expertise as credibility, not distance.
- Make complex topics like trauma, attachment, neurodivergence, nervous system regulation, and behavior feel understandable and usable.

**This site should not feel:**
Salesy, flashy, corporate, cold, generic, clinical in a detached way, punitive, compliance-focused, or like a motivational speaker site.

## Branding

**Visual identity defined:** partial

The current With Connection site is the brand source. The new site should align 100% with recognizable existing elements:

- Brand/company name: With Connection, LLC.
- Public brand language: "With Connection."
- Logo from the existing Squarespace site.
- Warm, relational, Portland-rooted identity.
- Existing image style, especially photography, book imagery, article imagery, and professional development visuals.
- Current palette signals found in the source site CSS: teal/blue-green accent around `#157c93`, dark charcoal around `#272727`, white, near-white `#f7f7f7`, and restrained neutrals.
- Current typography feel: serif headings similar to Adobe Garamond/Arno/Garamond Premier and sans-serif body/navigation similar to Proxima Nova.

The new site does not need to replicate Squarespace mechanics, but it should preserve the recognizable With Connection mood, language, and visual identity.

---

## Constraints

- Framework: Astro static output.
- Language: strict TypeScript.
- Styles: Tailwind CSS utilities.
- Deployment target: Cloudflare Pages unless changed later.
- Do not add client-side JavaScript unless an Astro island is explicitly justified.
- Do not add new dependencies without documenting them.
- Use existing With Connection site content as the launch source of truth.
- Primary conversion is an inquiry form; form handling provider is not yet chosen.
- No email gate for free resources.
- Owner/developer will maintain content after launch; updates will be frequent at first and taper off.
